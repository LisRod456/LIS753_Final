About Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli is a Japanese animation studio. The studio began in the late 80s, led by directors Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, along with producer Toshio Suzuki.

Most films also feature music scores written by composer Joe Hisaishi.
